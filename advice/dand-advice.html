Hi my name is Dan DeRienzo and I have some advice for you!

Don't be afraid to ask!

At first, web development can be a daunting endeavor. This does not meant that we should feel intimidated. When we need help, we should ask, without fear.

Those who have gone before us are normally quite willing to help - and it even makes them feel good and proud.

It is important to remain open.

As you progress in your learning, remember to remain receptive to advice from those at a higher level. It is easy for us to feel that we no longer need direction - but it is beneficial to all when we continue to learn as much as we can.

We should also begin to feel a desire to help those who are just strting out, as we once were. In this way, the cycle of knowledge can continue to remain strong.

If we all remain brave, receptive and considerate, we can do great things!
